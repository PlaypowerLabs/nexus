# Landing Page: Video Story

## Design Concept
A narrative-driven landing page that uses video and storytelling to engage visitors. Features include:
- Hero video background or embedded video player
- Story-based scrolling experience
- Character journey that explains the platform
- Testimonials from students/parents
- Before/after showcase of student progress

## Target Audience
- Parents making purchasing decisions
- Educators looking for classroom tools
- Older students (Grades 6-8) who appreciate storytelling

## Key Features
- Video player integration (YouTube/Vimeo embedded)
- Scroll-triggered animations
- Story timeline
- Student success stories
- Parent testimonials

## Content Needed
- 30-60 second explainer video
- Student testimonial videos
- Progress showcase videos
- Behind-the-scenes content

## Recommended Tools for Building
- Video.js for custom video player
- ScrollMagic for scroll animations
- AOS (Animate On Scroll)
- Cloudinary or Vimeo for video hosting

## Tone
- Inspirational and aspirational
- Focus on transformation and growth
- Emotional connection through stories
